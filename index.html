FAQ

Who is this for?

Companies, scale-ups, and organizations worldwide seeking funding support, ESG/CSR maturity, and cyber/ISO readiness.

How fast can we see results?

It depends on scope. Typically 1–2 weeks for assessment & roadmap, and 4–8+ weeks for full documentation and implementation support.

What do you need from us to start?

Business goals, basic operational/financial context, existing policies/processes, and (if available) current security posture or audit history.

Do you implement or only advise?

Both: from strategic design to hands-on documentation and coordination with third parties (institutions, auditors, security vendors).
